Why SenseFlow wins: These have no malicious links, so basic scanners pass them. RoBERTa will flag the anomalous NLP context, and RAG will match the "Invoice Spoofing" tactic.

"Hello HR, I recently changed my banking institution and need to update my direct deposit information for the upcoming payroll cycle. Can you please process this change manually today? I have attached my new voided check details."
